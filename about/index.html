About Me

Hi, my name is Tim Lee, a software engineer and a junior game producer. I like to play games and hope to build a good game for all. Sakurai Masahiro is my idol.

This blog comes to show you how I feel about popular games, and some technical articles about game and programming. I forget things sometimes, espacially those techs that I don’t use a lot. Hope it can helps you too.

If you want to make games that bring our life happiness, don’t hesitate to find me, I will be glad to join you.

If you need a game-mate, me too.
